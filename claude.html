Location

The Heart of Magna Graecia

Perched above the Ionian Sea near Syracuse — one of the most storied cities of the ancient world — Casa Maddalena places you at the crossroads of timeless culture and natural splendour.

  • Ortigia Island 5 min
  • Greek Theatre of Syracuse 15 min
  • Vendicari Nature Reserve 30 min
  • Noto — UNESCO Baroque City 40 min
  • Ragusa & Modica 60 min
